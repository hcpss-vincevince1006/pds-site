<p>PDS is part of The Redesign of Teacher Education (also known as The Redesign). Additional insights into the PDS program as it relates to the Redesign is outlined in <a href="http://www.marylandpublicschools.org/about/Documents/DEE/ProgramApproval/PDS/PDSImplementationManual.pdf" target="_blank">Professional Development Schools: An Implementation Manual</a> <i class="fa fa-file-pdf-o" aria-hidden="true"></i>, produced by the Maryland Partnership for Teaching and Learning, K-16 (Superintendents and Deans Committee). This manual provides the framework for The Redesign as well as descriptions of personnel, definitions of terminology, and developmental guidelines to be used for assessment.</p>
